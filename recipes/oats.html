Oats!

Description

A hearty meal; served both hot and cold!


Be sure to like and subscribe!


Ingredients

  • oats (dry)
  • water (cold)
  • sea salt

Steps

  1. pour water into pot
  2. sprinkle sea salt
  3. bring water to a boil
  4. pour in oats
  5. stir for three minutes
  6. serve and enjoy!
