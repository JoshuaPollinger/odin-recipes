Cheese Sandwich

[BACK TO MAIN PAGE]

Description

A cheesy meal; served both hot and cold on your favorite bread!

Be sure to like and subscribe!


Ingredients

  • bread
  • swiss cheese slice
  • mayonnaise
  • fresh pepper
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. spread mayo on bread
  2. grind fresh pepper on mayo
  3. sprinkle garlic powder on mayo
  4. add slice of cheese
  5. serve and enjoy!
